Our story

Welcome to FRESCO, where coffee is more than just a drink – it’s a passion. Located in the heart of Chennai, our shop is a cozy retreat where the aroma of freshly brewed coffee mingles with the sounds of good conversation.

Our journey began with a simple idea: to create a space where people can come together over exceptional coffee. We source our beans from the best farms around the world, ensuring that each cup is rich, flavorful, and ethically produced. Our skilled baristas take pride in their craft, whether they’re pouring a perfectly balanced espresso or creating a new specialty drink just for you.

But we’re more than just a place to get your coffee fix. FRESCO is a community hub, a place where you can meet friends, relax with a book, or simply enjoy a moment of peace. We’ve designed our space to be warm and inviting, with cozy corners, friendly faces, and a vibe that feels like home.

Join us at FRESCO – where every visit is an experience, and every cup is a celebration of coffee.
